Quick documentation

Operators

  • isa / someare

    pine isa tree
    or
    tree someare pine
    would mean: all pines are trees

  • madeof / partof

    tree madeof wood
    or
    wood partof tree
    would mean: all trees contain wood

  • contradict

    solid contradict liquid
    or
    liquid contradict solid
    would mean: if "ice isa solid", then it's impossible that "ice isa liquid"

  • help / helpedby

    water help organic_life_form
    or
    organic_life_form helpedby water
    would mean: water is good for every organic life form

  • make / madeby

    musician make music
    or
    music madeby musician
    would mean: all musicians make some music

  • destroy / destroyedby

    trans_fat destroy animal
    or
    animal destroyedby trans_fat
    would mean: trans_fat is harmful for every animal

  • largerthan / smallerthan

    start largerthan planet
    or
    planet smallerthan start
    would mean: all stars are larger than all planets

  • from / originof

    judo from japan
    or
    japan originof judo
    would mean: judo was invented in japan

    human from ape
    or
    ape originof human
    would mean: ape is an ancestor of human

  • like / likedby

    philatelist like stamp
    or
    stamp likedby philatelist
    would mean: all philatelists like stamps (but not necessarly all stamps)

  • promote / promotedby

    socrates promote skepticism
    or
    skepticism promotedby socrates
    would mean: socrates promotes the ideology of skepticism

Keywords

  • not

    pine not isa animal
    remove connection "pine isa animal"

  • whatis

    whatis pine
    ask for a definition of pine (will only output direct connections)

  • define

    define pine
    ask for a longer definition of pine (will output both direct and implicit connections)

  • why / how

    why pine madeof water
    or
    how pine madeof water
    ask the AI more details on why/how pine is made of water

  • which

    pine isa tree which isa plant madeof wood which isa organic_lifeform which madeof water which isa liquid madeof oxygen and hydrogen
    consider the last complement as current subject

  • you / me

    me isa human
    you isa program
    keywords that replace you and the AI you are chatting with

  • and

    tree isa plant madeof trunk, leaf and branch
    join multiple statements with the "and" keyword

  • rename

    rename circle round
    changes the name of circle to round

  • aliasof / unalias

    circle aliasof round
    or
    circle unalias round
    make round be the same thing as circle, or not

  • what

    pine madeof what
    ask the AI for a partial definition of pine

  • start / stop

    start or stop conversational mode with AI

  • thinkabout

    thinkabout pine
    the AI will try to make a theory about pine

  • think

    the AI will try to make a theory about something

  • yes/no

    yes
    or
    no
    confirm or unconfirm the AI's latest theory

  • askabout

    askabout pine
    the AI will ask a question about pine

  • ask

    the AI will ask a question

  • teachabout

    teachabout pine
    the AI will teach something about pine

  • teach

    the AI will teach something

  • talkabout

    talkabout pine
    the AI will teach, ask, or make a theory about pine

  • talk

    the AI will teach, ask, or make a theory

  • testmemory

    test memory for inconsistencies and incoherences
